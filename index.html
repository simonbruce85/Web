For3x Funding Roadmap

This project will consist in 5 different phases listed below

Phase I

April 1st, 2022 to April 4, 2022

First Pre-sale will take place. In this phase, only whitelisted people will be able to participate.

The first 30 pieces will be available for mint at a super special price of 0.02ETH

Phase II

April 5, 2022 to April 8, 2022

The second Pre-sale will take place. 50 pieces will be available for mint at 0.03ETH

In this phase, the pieces will not be revealed yet

Phase III

April 8, 2021 to April 15, 2021

This phase will be the public sale, where the 120 remaining NFTs will be available for mint.

After this phase, all the NFTs will be revealed, and the draw and competition processes will officially start

Phase IV

Dec 28, 2020 to Jan 29, 2020

The draw will take place, and the winners of the first six 10k forex funded accounts will be announced on our social media

Phase V

April 25, 2021 to Nov 16, 2021

The organization of the tournament will be announced and will start the four-week competition

Final Phase

Dec 28, 2020 to Jan 29, 2020

The four winners of the tournaments will be announced, and their 10k forex funded accounts will be assigned

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the objectives of this project?

The objective of this project is to provide the opportunity of being funded to those traders who have been close to passing a funding challenge, cannot afford a funding challenge fee, or any other person interested in operating in the Forex market with a remarkably reliable broker

What is a NFT sale and pre-sale?

Pre-sale and Sale will be the two different selling phases of our project, where the traders will be able to acquire their pieces until the time finishes or supply lasts

What is the pre-sale start date?

To be determined

How may I access to pre-sale?

Pre-sale will be the first selling phase of our project, and all the traders have to do is be one of the first 80 people to mint your NFT before the deadline. Please follow our social media for announcements
